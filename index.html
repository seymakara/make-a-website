My Journey

Vienna, Austria

Home of Beethoven and Brahms and Haydn and Mozart and Schubert and Strauss. Very nice cafes and museums. History is everwhere.

Stockholm, Sweden

One of the most beautiful cities in Europe for sightseeing. Venice of North (set on 14 islands). This is where IKEA and Vikings are from :)

Rome, Italy

Great monuments at every corner, astonishing artistic heritage, city like an open-air museum, the best espresso and, best of all, it is a heaven for a foodie.

Berlin, Germany

A truly inspiring city with its lively artistic culture. Besides, world-class museums, you can feel the fascinating historical background even when walking aimlessly on the streets.

Nice, France

A perfect Mediterranean experience with its unique cuisine, beaches, old town, bazaars, fields of lavender, ancient olive groves, cliff-top roads.

Monaco

It can be world's second smallest city but definitely the best place to live the good life. Even Mc Donalds has a fascinating sea view.

Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina

One of unique place to experience the blended culture of East, West and a heartbreaking civil war. The city has splendid natural beauties; mountainscapes, rivers and immense greenery.

New York City, USA

The USA is one of the most fulfilling travel destinations. It offers travellers a range of natural beauty; mountains, beaches, rainforest, deserts, canyons even glaciers.

Kyoto, Japan,

My favorite country among all others I've been to. This country has everything to satisfy you as a tourist. It is definitely timeless and a must-visit place

Taroko Gorge, Taiwan

The country has the most amazing landscapes. It also has an intriguing cuisine with really exciting ingredients. I am not a big fan of it though :)

Singapore

Singapore is like a beaufilly designed city from the game Simcity. There is nothing unattractive in this city. It seems like a place out of this world.

Bali, Indonesia

Bali has everything for a perfect holiday with beutiful beaches, forest, rice terraces, volcanoes, perfect spot for diving, exciting night life, world class spa centers and unique temples.
